Risks

In order to utilize our Services, you must collect the appropriate biological sample(s) for the test(s). Our tests require the collection of a blood sample. Although the risks and discomforts associated with a finger prick blood draw are very low, you might be at risk for excessive bleeding, fainting, feeling light-headed, bruising, hematoma (blood accumulating under the skin), or infection (a slight risk any time the skin is broken). If you have a history of excessive bleeding or fainting while having blood work done, we advise avoiding our tests as they require self-collection of a blood sample.

Disclosing certain information may make you uncomfortable. Our Services include protein tests that may reveal sensitive information about your health. This information may be distressing.

Health information that you choose to share with your physician or other healthcare providers may become part of your medical record and through that route may be accessible to other healthcare providers and/or insurance companies in the future.
